# Vibes

Pressed Hub should feel quick and calm to live in, with the chrome staying out of the way until you need it.

The sidebar should behave more like a smart rail than a permanent wall: always available, visually lightweight, and able to open up the moment someone wants to navigate.

Shared shell changes should stay respectful of the app surfaces that already want their own immersive canvas, especially the full-screen app routes.

When we snapshot or seed the product, the repo should still feel safe to ship: realistic demo data is good, but live connector credentials leaking into dumps breaks trust and slows momentum.
